How is it going with Ni'mah?

Startup Entrepreneurship Tech

A quick reflection on my experience with Ni'mah so far. The good, the bad, and the exciting future ahead!

Introduction

What is Ni'mah? Ni'mah is an idea, an idea that me and my team plan to improve and turn into a successful startup. In this blog post, I'll share my journey with Ni'mah so far, including the challenges we've faced, the milestones we've achieved, and our vision for the future.

The problem Ni'mah is solving?

Ni'mah aims to address the issue of food waste and hunger by connecting surplus food from restaurants and open buffets with people in need. Our platform facilitates the efficient redistribution of food, ensuring that excess food reaches those who can benefit from it, rather than going to waste.

Figure 1: left over foods at most open buffets (after buffet closes)

How does Ni'mah Solve this?

Ni'mah operates through a user-friendly mobile application that allows restaurants to list their surplus food and individuals in need to claim it. Here are the key steps involved in our process:

  1. Food Listing: Restaurants and buffet providers can easily list their surplus food items on the Ni'mah app, specifying details such as quantity, type of food, and pickup time.
  2. Food Claiming: Individuals in need can browse available food listings on the app and claim items that suit their preferences and dietary requirements.
  3. Pickup Coordination: Once a food item is claimed, the app facilitates communication between the restaurant and the claimant to arrange a convenient pickup time and location.
  4. Feedback and Ratings: After the food has been picked up, both parties can provide feedback and ratings to ensure quality and reliability within the Ni'mah community.

Key Benefits of implementing Ni'mah?

  • Addressing Hunger: Ni'mah provides a practical solution to hunger by making nutritious food accessible to individuals who may otherwise struggle to afford it.
  • Community Engagement: The platform fosters a sense of community and social responsibility by encouraging restaurants and individuals to contribute to a worthy cause.
  • Meet SDGs 2 & 12: Ni'mah clearly addresses SDGs 2 & 12 which is concerned with ending hunger and reducing waste (espescially food waste)
  • Meet Qatar Vision 2030: By promoting sustainable practices and social welfare, Ni'mah aligns with Qatar's Vision 2030 goals of building a diversified and sustainable economy while enhancing the quality of life for its citizens.

What is my role in this?

As the CTO and co-founder of Ni'mah, I am responsible for overseeing the technical development of our platform. This includes managing the development team, ensuring the app's functionality and user experience, and implementing scalable solutions to support our growth.

Am I an Expert in this field? Not at all, but I am learning a lot along the way and I am passionate about making a positive impact through technology.

Conclusion

My journey with Ni'mah has been both challenging and rewarding. While we have faced obstacles along the way, our commitment to addressing food waste and hunger remains unwavering. I am excited about the future of Ni'mah and the potential it holds to make a meaningful difference in our community.

Stay tuned for more updates as we continue to develop and expand Ni'mah!
